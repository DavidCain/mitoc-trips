Other Discounts

College Club Program

To receive a discount for the 2026-27 season, fill out a brief form.

MITOC student members can get discounted passes. The price is significantly cheaper than the usual young adult rate.

The major distinction between the Ikon (Full) and Base passes are that the Base passes have some blackout dates, and provide 5 days vs. 7 days at the non-core resorts. In the Northeast, locations include:

  • Stratton, VT (unlimited)
  • Sugarbush, VT (unlimited)
  • Tremblant, QC (unlimited)
  • Killington-Pico, VT
  • Sugarloaf, ME
  • Sunday River, ME
  • Loon, NH

Discounts can be requested now and used later; the program closes December 1, 2026, so make sure to purchase before then. Note that prices are expected to increase mid-spring and mid-fall; the exact dates of the price increases are unknown.

Please direct any questions to mitoc.ikon@gmail.com.
