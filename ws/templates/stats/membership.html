Membership Statistics

These visualizations are meant to give quick insights. Feel free to download the raw data to explore further.

MITOC members

MITOC members are defined as those who:

  • Have paid current annual dues
  • Are MIT students, or have ever participated on a trip
(This excludes people who only use the club for rentals or MITOC discounts)

MITOC members grouped

This is the same member definition as above, but with affiliations grouped for easy visualization of the key "groups" in MITOC.

Current Leaders

Affiliation of people who hold a leader rating, even if they never lead trips.

Leaders

Current affiliation of people who have ever led a trip (even if they're not currently rated).

All dues-paying members

This is a count of all MITOCers who've paid their dues (but may not have rented gear, participated on trips, or otherwise made use of the club).

Just renters

People who pay dues and rent gear, but have never been on a trip.

Just discounts

People who use MITOC discounts, but have never rented gear or been on a trip.
