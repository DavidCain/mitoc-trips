Mentorship

Mentoring relationships are an indispensable part of how MITOC members develop into awesome leaders. To ensure everyone has an opportunity to make these important connections, the Winter School Chairs will match new and experienced leaders who indicate interest in this program. Mentor/mentee assignments will be announced in advance of the Leaders Retreat. Email ws-chair@mit.edu with questions.

 
Mentor expectations
  • Meet your mentee before Winter School begins (ideally at WS retreat)
  • Be responsive to questions from your mentee throughout WS
  • Lead a trip with your mentee in the first weekend of WS*
  • Provide your mentee with constructive feedback about their trip planning, group management, and decision making skills
  • Provide WSC with rating upgrade recommendation as appropriate

*Note: Skiing and Ice climbing mentor/mentee pairs can lead their trip on the second weekend.
