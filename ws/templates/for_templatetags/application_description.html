Please complete and submit the form below if you're interested in becoming a MITOC Hiking Leader.

The leader ratings are assigned based on a combination of your involvement with MITOC and leadership experience in addition to your baseline technical skills of hiking in the Whites.

The requirements are as follows:

  • Co-Leader: Participant in two MITOC hiking events. Must be different weekends.
  • Leader: Co-lead two official MITOC hikes (on separate weekends). On one of the hikes, handle the majority of the planning and group management, and get a recommendation from the leader.
